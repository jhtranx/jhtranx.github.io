SUPER NATURAL SUPER MARKET - CONCEPT ART

Role Time Frame Tools and Methods
Artist December 2019 Charcoal,
Pen/Pencil/Paper,
Adobe Photoshop

Overview

I wanted to picture what a would happen when a regular location, such as a supermarket, is transformed by an abstract force. Some of the techniques I used are charcoal negation, cross hatching, blind contour, texture, and two point perspective. I used mechanical pencil, graphite pencil, chamois, blending stump, charcoal, and eraser.
